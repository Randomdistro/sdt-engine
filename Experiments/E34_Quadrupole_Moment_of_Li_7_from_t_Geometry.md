## 1. Background

Li-7 = α + t (alpha + triton). The triton cap creates a prolate shape.

Li-7's large quadrupole moment (-4.01 fm²) arises from the asymmetric alpha+triton arrangement.

## 3. Standard Prediction

Standard: shell model explains it from p₃/₂ shell deformation.
