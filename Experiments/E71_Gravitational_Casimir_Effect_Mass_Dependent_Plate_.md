# E71: Gravitational Casimir Effect: Mass-Dependent Plate Force

> **Category**: Vacuum / Medium
> **Difficulty**: MODERATE
> **Status**: Proposed (laboratory)

---

## 1. Background

SDT predicts the gravitational (convergent pressure) component of the Casimir force depends on mass.

## 2. SDT Prediction

Casimir plates made of different materials should show slightly different force due to their different nuclear occlusion fractions.

## 3. Standard Prediction

Standard: Casimir force depends only on plate geometry and dielectric properties.

## 4. Observable

Measure Casimir force between tungsten plates vs aluminium plates at identical gaps.

## 5. Apparatus

AFM Casimir apparatus with interchangeable plates.

## 6. Feasibility

MODERATE — requires precision material control.

## 7. Impact

- **If confirmed**: Mass-dependent Casimir force would prove the gravitational component.
- **If not confirmed**: If no material dependence: Casimir force is purely electromagnetic.
