# E50: Cosmic Neutrino Background Temperature

> **Category**: Cosmology
> **Difficulty**: HIGH
> **Status**: Awaiting detector

---

## 1. Background

Neutrinos decoupled earlier than photons. SDT's lattice model may predict a different decoupling temperature.

## 2. SDT Prediction

SDT predicts T_ν ≠ 1.95 K because neutrinos decouple at a different point in the lattice pressure history.

## 3. Standard Prediction

Standard: T_ν = (4/11)^(1/3) × T_CMB ≈ 1.95 K from entropy conservation.

## 4. Observable

Measure T_CνB when PTOLEMY or successor detector becomes available.

## 5. Apparatus

PTOLEMY tritium detector.

## 6. Feasibility

HIGH — frontier detector.

## 7. Impact

- **If confirmed**: Incorrect T_ν would distinguish lattice from expansion cosmology.
- **If not confirmed**: If T_ν = 1.95 K: consistent with standard decoupling.
