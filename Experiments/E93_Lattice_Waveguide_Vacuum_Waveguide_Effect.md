# E93: Lattice Waveguide: Vacuum Waveguide Effect

> **Category**: Electromagnetic
> **Difficulty**: MODERATE
> **Status**: Proposed (laboratory)

---

## 1. Background

SDT predicts the same waveguide modes because boundary conditions are identical. But predicts subtle corrections from lattice granularity at extreme frequencies.

## 2. SDT Prediction

A hollow tube in vacuum should guide electromagnetic waves differently from free-space propagation due to lattice boundary effects.

## 3. Standard Prediction

Standard: waveguide theory from boundary conditions on EM fields.

## 4. Observable

Measure waveguide cutoff frequencies at extreme precision in vacuum.

## 5. Apparatus

Microwave/mm-wave waveguide in vacuum.

## 6. Feasibility

MODERATE.

## 7. Impact

- **If confirmed**: Any deviation from EM theory at extreme frequencies indicates lattice granularity.
- **If not confirmed**: If no deviation: lattice is smooth at measured frequencies.
