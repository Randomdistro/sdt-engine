> **Category**: Gravity / Occlusion

## 1. Background

SDT's convergence model should reproduce Newton's tidal formula at leading order but predict additional small corrections.

## 2. SDT Prediction

Tidal forces arise from convergence gradient differentials, not gravitational potential gradients. SDT predicts subtle higher-order tidal harmonics.

## 3. Standard Prediction

Standard: tidal forces from gravitational potential gradient.

## 4. Observable

Analyse high-precision ocean tide gauge data for SDT-predicted higher harmonics.

## 5. Apparatus

Global tide gauge network (UHSLC).

## 6. Feasibility

LOW — archival.

## 7. Impact

- **If confirmed**: Detection of SDT-predicted harmonics in tidal data.
- **If not confirmed**: If standard harmonics suffice: convergence corrections are undetectable.
