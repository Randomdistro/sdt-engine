# E38: EC Rate vs Charge State for Heavy Elements

> **Category**: Nuclear Physics
> **Difficulty**: HIGH
> **Status**: Proposed (GSI/FAIR)

---

## 1. Background

SDT's He-3 core mechanism predicts charge-state dependence is universal. Cr-51, Mn-54, Fe-55 should all show rate cessation at full ionisation.

## 2. SDT Prediction

EC rate drops to zero at full ionisation for ALL elements, not just Be-7.

## 3. Standard Prediction

Standard: EC requires bound electrons; rate varies with electron density at nucleus.

## 4. Observable

Measure EC rates of Cr-51, Mn-54, Fe-55 at various charge states in EBIT or storage ring.

## 5. Apparatus

GSI/FAIR storage ring, NIST EBIT.

## 6. Feasibility

HIGH — frontier capability.

## 7. Impact

- **If confirmed**: Universal charge-state EC cessation validates the He-3 core mechanism.
- **If not confirmed**: If some elements show EC at full ionisation: mechanism is more complex.
