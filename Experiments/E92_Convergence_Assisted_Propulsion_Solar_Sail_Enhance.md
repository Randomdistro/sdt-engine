# E92: Convergence-Assisted Propulsion: Solar Sail Enhancement

> **Category**: Engineering
> **Difficulty**: HIGH
> **Status**: Proposed (space mission)

---

## 1. Background

SDT predicts the convergence field contributes additional pressure beyond photon momentum. A properly designed sail might harvest this.

## 2. SDT Prediction

A sail designed to reflect convergent pressure (not just photon pressure) should experience enhanced thrust.

## 3. Standard Prediction

Standard: solar sail thrust = radiation pressure only.

## 4. Observable

Deploy a solar sail with convergence-optimised surface (dense, reflective) and compare thrust to photon-pressure predictions.

## 5. Apparatus

Solar sail spacecraft (JAXA/NASA heritage).

## 6. Feasibility

HIGH — space mission.

## 7. Impact

- **If confirmed**: Excess thrust beyond photon pressure would demonstrate convergence harvesting.
- **If not confirmed**: If thrust matches photon pressure: convergence contribution is undetectable at sail scale.
