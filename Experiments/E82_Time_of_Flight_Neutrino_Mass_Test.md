# E82: Time of Flight Neutrino Mass Test

> **Category**: Nuclear Physics
> **Difficulty**: HIGH
> **Status**: Awaiting nearby SN

---

## 1. Background

SDT predicts neutrinos are massless lattice modes. Speed deficits arise from dispersion.

## 2. SDT Prediction

Neutrinos travel at c (they are lattice distortions, not massive particles). Any measured speed deficit is lattice dispersion, not rest mass.

## 3. Standard Prediction

Standard: neutrinos have small mass (< 0.8 eV) and travel slightly below c.

## 4. Observable

Measure neutrino speed from next galactic supernova with sub-ns timing.

## 5. Apparatus

IceCube + SN optical detection.

## 6. Feasibility

HIGH — requires nearby supernova.

## 7. Impact

- **If confirmed**: If ν speed = c exactly: supports massless lattice mode.
- **If not confirmed**: If v < c: neutrinos have mass, requiring SDT modification.
