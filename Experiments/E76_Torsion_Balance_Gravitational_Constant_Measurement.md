# E76: Torsion Balance Gravitational Constant Measurement

> **Category**: Gravity / Occlusion
> **Difficulty**: MODERATE
> **Status**: Proposed (laboratory)

---

## 1. Background

The ~0.05% discrepancy between different G measurements may reflect real environmental variation.

## 2. SDT Prediction

G is not fundamental — it is derived from G = c²R/(k²r) evaluated at laboratory scale. Different lab environments may give slightly different G.

## 3. Standard Prediction

Standard: G is a universal fundamental constant (6.674 × 10⁻¹¹).

## 4. Observable

Measure G in underground laboratories vs mountaintop vs satellite. Look for systematic environment dependence.

## 5. Apparatus

Multiple torsion balance facilities.

## 6. Feasibility

MODERATE — coordinated measurement campaign.

## 7. Impact

- **If confirmed**: If G varies with environment: proves G is derived, not fundamental.
- **If not confirmed**: If G is constant: environmental effects are below measurement precision.
