# E65: Stellar Main Sequence from k-Gradient Equilibrium

> **Category**: Stellar Physics
> **Difficulty**: LOW
> **Status**: Archival analysis

---

## 1. Background

SDT maps the main sequence to the k-hierarchy: hot blue stars have low k (strong convergence), cool red stars have high k (weak convergence).

## 2. SDT Prediction

The HR diagram main sequence is a k-gradient equilibrium sequence. Each spectral type corresponds to a specific k-value.

## 3. Standard Prediction

Standard: main sequence from mass-luminosity relation and nuclear burning.

## 4. Observable

Map measured stellar k-values (from orbital velocities) to HR diagram positions.

## 5. Apparatus

GAIA stellar survey data.

## 6. Feasibility

LOW — archival.

## 7. Impact

- **If confirmed**: k-hierarchy mapping of the main sequence validates the pressure model.
- **If not confirmed**: If no correlation: k-values don't map cleanly to spectral type.
