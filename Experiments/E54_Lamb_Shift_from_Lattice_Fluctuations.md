# E54: Lamb Shift from Lattice Fluctuations

> **Category**: Electromagnetic
> **Difficulty**: HIGH
> **Status**: Open problem

---

## 1. Background

SDT replaces virtual particle loops with real lattice fluctuations.

## 2. SDT Prediction

The Lamb shift in hydrogen arises from spation lattice fluctuations ("vacuum fluctuations" in QED are lattice thermal noise).

## 3. Standard Prediction

Standard: Lamb shift from vacuum polarisation and electron self-energy in QED.

## 4. Observable

Calculate the Lamb shift from the spation lattice fluctuation spectrum.

## 5. Apparatus

Computational.

## 6. Feasibility

HIGH — requires lattice fluctuation model.

## 7. Impact

- **If confirmed**: Reproducing Lamb shift from real lattice fluctuations would reinterpret QED.
- **If not confirmed**: If shift differs: lattice fluctuation model needs refinement.
