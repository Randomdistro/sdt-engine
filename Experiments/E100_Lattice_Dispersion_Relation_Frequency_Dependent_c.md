## 1. Background

SDT predicts the lattice relay mechanism introduces dispersion at ~Planck frequencies. Measurable proxies: gamma-ray burst arrival times at different energies.

## 2. SDT Prediction

At extremely high frequencies (approaching Planck frequency), c should show dispersion due to lattice granularity.

## 3. Standard Prediction

Standard: c is frequency-independent in vacuum (Lorentz invariance).

## 4. Observable

Measure arrival time differences of high- vs low-energy photons from GRBs.

## 5. Apparatus

Fermi-LAT, CTA.

## 6. Feasibility

LOW — data exists.

## 7. Impact

- **If confirmed**: Frequency-dependent c would prove lattice granularity and set the lattice spacing.
- **If not confirmed**: If no dispersion: Lorentz invariance holds to measured precision.
