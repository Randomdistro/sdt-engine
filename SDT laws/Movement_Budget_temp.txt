All of special relativity follows.AbstractWe prove that the constraint vcirc2 + vtrans2 = c2 follows from three axioms about the spation relay lattice: a hard speed bound on deformation propagation (M1), the identification of matter as circulating deformation (M2), and the orthogonality of circulation and translation (M3).
