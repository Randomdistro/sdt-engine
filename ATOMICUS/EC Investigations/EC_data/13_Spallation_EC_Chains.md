Pattern: ALL six double-EC elements have k/α⁻¹ > 1.3 (loose coupling).
None are noble gas closures. This is consistent with the observation
that loose structures are more susceptible to spallation damage —
tightly coupled noble-gas scaffolds resist neutron ejection.
