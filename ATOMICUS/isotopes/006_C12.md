### Children

### The CNO Branch Point

C-12 is the **catalyst** of the CNO cycle: it captures protons, processes them through nitrogen and oxygen intermediates, and returns to C-12 having produced one alpha from 4 protons. The cycle works precisely BECAUSE C-12's pure-deuteron symmetry makes it a geometrically efficient proton receptor.
